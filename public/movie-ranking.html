The Top 3 Movies of All Time

"Forest Gump"

Forest Gump is an inspiring movie about overcoming adversity. Also, it is a beautiful love Story

Movie Number 2:

"The Hobbit"

The Hobbit is a Fantasy adventure film that is very entertaining also imaginiative world where anything is possible I Enjoy this film most of all because it helps me just get away from reality it helps me unwind from the mental stresses of life kind of like a vacation for the mind.

Movie Number 3:

"The Theory of Everthing

The Theory of Everything is a 2014 biographical romantic drama film directed by James Marsh. Set at the University of Cambridge, it details the life of the theoretical physicist Stephen Hawking.
